# Brand Identity

Welcome to our mini brand guide. Please ensure this information is followed and if anything is unclear, please email [hello@vunked.co.uk](mailto:hello@vunked.co.uk) for further clarification.

# 1. Overview

**Mission Statement:**

Simplifying campervan electrics for everyone, making it accessible, stress-free, and empowering.

**Vision Statement:**

To become the go-to resource for campervan electrics, inspiring people to create their dream campervans with confidence.

**Core Values:**

- Simplicity: Make complex systems understandable.
- Empowerment: Provide tools and knowledge to DIYers.
- Helpfulness: Always go above and beyond to assist our customers.
- Innovation: Use technology to improve the campervan conversion process.

**Target Audience:**

- Aspiring campervan converters
- Outdoor enthusiasts, DIYers, and remote workers seeking freedom on the road.

# 2. **Visual Identity**

### **Logo Guidelines**

- **Primary Logo:** A clean and modern logo featuring a campervan icon integrated with a subtle electrical symbol.
- **Variations:**
    - Full-color version for digital use.
    - Monochrome version for print.
    - Icon-only for social media or compact spaces.
- **Usage Rules:**
    - Maintain a clear space of at least 20px around the logo.
    - Do not stretch, distort, or recolor the logo.

Logo files can be downloaded [**here**](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1i5XwYW_1bkkjl0vCGH8KKZ48pWmhjsDh?usp=sharing)

### **Color Palette**

- **Primary Colours:**
    - Burnt Orange: #E76F51
    - Navy Blue: #264653
    - White: #FFFFFF
- **Accent Colors:**
    - Blue - lighter: #366476
    - Blue - lightest: #468299

### **Typography**

- **Headings:** Montserrat - [https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat) (Arial when not available)
- **Body Text:** Montserrat - [https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat) (Arial when not available)
- **Size Hierarchy:**
    - H1: 36px
    - H2: 28px
    - Body: 16px

### **Imagery and Photography**

- **Style:**
    - Bright, outdoor settings showcasing campervan lifestyles.
    - Photos should evoke adventure and confidence.
    - Images featuring hands-on work with electrics are encouraged.
    - Educational infographics
